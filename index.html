Welcome to Earthafying 🌍

Earthafying exists to bring people together across cultures and borders — one shared moment at a time. We're all from here. To keep this a kind, safe place, everyone agrees to a few simple things.

Community Guidelines

  • Be kind. Treat everyone with respect, whoever and wherever they are.
  • Everyone here is 13 or older. If you're under 13, you can't use Earthafying yet.
  • Share your own world. Post your own photos and moments — not other people's private information.
  • Keep it safe for all ages. No nudity, sexual content, or anything that sexualises a person — and absolutely nothing that sexualises or endangers a minor.
  • No harm. No bullying, harassment, hate, threats, violence, or content that encourages self-harm.
  • No spam, scams, or illegal content.
  • Adults and young people. Adults must never use Earthafying to contact or pressure a minor inappropriately. This is taken extremely seriously.
  • Speak up. If something worries you, look out for each other and report it. We review concerns with human care.

Breaking these guidelines can lead to content being removed or an account being closed.

Terms (the plain version)

  • You're responsible for what you post and how you behave here.
  • You keep ownership of your photos. By posting, you give Earthafying permission to display them inside the app so others can see your moment.
  • Earthafying is offered free, as-is, during this early phase — things may change or break as it grows.
  • We may update these guidelines and terms. Continuing to use Earthafying means you accept the latest version.

Your privacy (short summary)

  • We collect your name, email, date of birth, and the moments you choose to post.
  • We use this only to run Earthafying — your account, the feed, and connections.
  • We do not sell your data, and Earthafying shows no ads.
  • Your data is stored securely through our service provider.
  • You can ask to delete your account and your data at any time.

This is an early version written in plain language. Before Earthafying opens to the public, a full, professionally reviewed policy will be provided.
